About Us

Authorized Siemens Innomotics partner delivering world-class motion & automation solutions with fast local support across coastal Karnataka.

Company Overview

Cureifyinn LLP is an authorized dealer for Siemens Innomotics products serving the coastal Karnataka region (Mangalore, Udupi and Karwar). We deliver industrial motors, drives and motion solutions tailored to manufacturing, process industries, marine, water & wastewater, and infrastructure projects.

Our focus is on reliable supply, fast local support, and engineering-led solutions that reduce downtime and improve energy efficiency.

To empower industries across coastal Karnataka with world-class Siemens Innomotics motion & automation products and rapid, expert local service enabling higher productivity, lower lifecycle cost and safer operations.
